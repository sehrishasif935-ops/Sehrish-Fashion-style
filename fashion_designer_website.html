My Story

A Passion for
Artistry & Craft

Born and raised in the cultural heart of Lahore, I grew up surrounded by the rich textile traditions of Pakistan — the hand-embroidered dupattas, the intricate zari work, the vibrant colors of festive celebrations.

After graduating from the National College of Arts with distinction, I honed my craft across Pakistan and internationally, before returning home to launch my own atelier. Every piece I create is a conversation between heritage and the contemporary woman.
